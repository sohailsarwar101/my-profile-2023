How it works:

A browser based simple yet powerful, easy to use screen recorder that process videos just in browser. Don't worry about poor network connection and time to download your recorded video, everythig is in your browser, record and save your screen quickly and easily.

Privacy Concerns:

It doesn't save or process any type of data/information on the server side, it just works in between user and user's browser. No worries about your privacy consurns, it's just good to go.
